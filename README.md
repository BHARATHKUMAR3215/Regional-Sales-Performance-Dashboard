Regional Sales Business Dashboard

Overview : The Regional Sales Business Dashboard is a Power BI project that provides insights into sales performance across different regions. It helps businesses monitor key metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations.

Objectives :
Analyze sales and profit performance by region,
Track monthly sales trends,
Identify top-performing products and customers,
Compare regional performance,
Support business decision-making with interactive dashboards.

Tools Used:
Power BI,
Power Query,
DAX
Excel/CSV Data Sources.

Key Metrics:
Total Sales,
Total Profit,
Total Orders,
Average Order Value,
Profit Margin %.

Dashboard Features:
Regional Sales Analysis,
Profit Analysis,
Monthly Sales Trends,
Product Category Performance,
Top Customers Analysis,
Interactive Filters and Slicers,

Business Insights:
Identified high-revenue regions,
Evaluated profitability across regions and categories,
Analyzed customer purchasing patterns,
Monitored sales growth over time.
